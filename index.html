How It Works!

1. Buy

Purchase a pass from our Bike Trac app or join us for an annual membership to access our bikes.

2. Unlock

Once you have a pass you can unlock your ride with the five digit code displayed on our app.

3. Ride

Enjoy your station-to-station ride while your pass is active!

4. Repeat

Once you have reached your destination simply lock your bike and return for your next trip!

Membership & Pricing

For short term passes or long term membership passes, we can help find the right pass for you!

No Membership

  • £3.25 Single Trip (One-way trip)
  • £7 Day Pass (24-hour access)
  • £15 3-Day Pass (72-hour access)
  • Overage Fees: £4 For Every Extra 30 Minutes

ANNUAL 30 MEMBERSHIP

£99

  • Join as an Annual 30 Member for only £99 per year. Enjoy multiple 30-minute, station-to-station trips all year round for less than £10 a month or as little as 30p a day
  • *The first 30 minutes of each ride are included in the membership. After 30 minutes extra usage fees apply, see Overage Fees below.

ANNUAL 45 MEMBERSHIP

£115

  • Join as an Annual 45 Member for only £115 per year. Enjoy unlimited 45 minute, station-to-station trips all year round.
  • *The first 45 minutes of each ride are included in the membership. After 45 minutes extra usage fees apply, see Overage Fees below.

*For every trip exceeding trip time, overage fees will accumulate at a rate of £4 for each 30 minutes of additional trip time. To avoid an overage charge, before your 30 or 45 minute trip is up, dock the bike and unlock another to continue riding.
